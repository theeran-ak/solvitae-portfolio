Who We Are

At Solvitae, we are a technology-driven startup dedicated to creating solutions that tackle real-world societal challenges. Using hardware, software and AI-powered systems, we strive to improve the quality of life, empower communities and make a meaningful impact in healthcare, personal safety, accessibility and sustainable living.
